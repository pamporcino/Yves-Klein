De Duve, Thierry. "Yves Klein, or The Dead Dealer." Translated by Rosalind Krauss. October 49 (Summer 1989): 72-90.

Restany, Pierre, and Klaus Ottmann. Yves Klein: Fire at the Heart of the Void. Putnam, CT: Spring Publications, 2005.
